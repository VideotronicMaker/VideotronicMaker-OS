# VideotronicMaker-OS Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment
The VideotronicMaker-OS (VTM OS) is a professional environment dedicated to hands-on learning and technical mastery. We are committed to fostering a community where members interact with integrity, respect, and a focus on constructive growth.

## Community Standards
To maintain a high-quality environment for all makers, we require adherence to the following standards:

* **Professional Language:** We do not tolerate the use of profanity, swearing, or offensive language in any community space. Communication must remain professional and respectful at all times.
* **Constructive Interaction:** While feedback is welcome, being unnecessarily critical or dismissive is prohibited. We focus on technical solutions and mutual encouragement.
* **Documentation-First Approach:** To respect the time of the maintainers and the community, participants are expected to read the provided documentation and utilize AI assistants (such as Google Gemini, Claude, or ChatGPT) for initial troubleshooting before seeking manual support.
* **Respect for Contributions:** This is a free, open-source repository provided for the benefit of the community. Please tryu to be understanding and considerate regarding the labor, time, and expertise shared here.
* **Efficient Inquiry:** Please avoid re-opening topics or issues that have already been addressed. Users are expected to search previous discussions, closed issues, and documentation before starting new threads.

## Enforcement
Community leaders reserve the right to remove comments or restrict access for those who violate these standards. Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the community leaders.
